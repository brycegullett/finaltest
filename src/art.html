Album Art


Client: Dr Cycle


    Role: Creative direction, design


    Deliverables:

    • Brand idenity

    • Distribution to music streaming services

The band Dr Cycle desired surrealistic approach to their upcoming releases in the form of graphic art. By working collaboratively with the artist, the idea was drafted to have a bizzare orb with a peculiar face. Having the orb character throughout all the art pieces ties their collection together.

During the creation my role was to organize the bands ideas, as well as give mockups to help guide to the final decisions on each piece of art.
