CD Design

Client: Crosley Court


    Team:

    • Alfred Taylor. - Graphic Artist

    • Bryce Gullett. - Graphic Design


    Deliverables:

    • Brand idenity

    • Product Design

    • Physical CD

The band Crosley Court desired to create a product that could bring their cohesive themes together for this particular album. By working collaboratively with the artist, the idea was drafted to convey the story of the record's concept and mood.

During the CD design my role was to construct its typography and layout, as well as the entirety of the CD-ROM design. Towards the end of this project, I was able to portray its emotions and lore by connecting the album artwork with the formatting.
