Shirts


Client: Crosley Court


    Role: Creative direction, design


    Deliverables:

    • Brand idenity

    • Product Design

    • Physical Shirts

The band Crosley Court desired to create a product line of shirts for their fans to purchase. The band wanted shirts that could match with their different themes. This included shirts with lyrics, and imagery from some of their songs.

My role was to create bold and eye catching shirt designs that fulfilled the bands' needs. Communicating with the band, as well as sending mockups helped make this line of products happen in a timely manner.
