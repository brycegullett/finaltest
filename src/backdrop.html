Stage Backdrop


Client: Crosley Court


    Team:

    • Jim Gullett. - Painter

    • NorthCoast Banners & Bags. - Printing Company

    • Bryce Gullett. - Graphic Design


    Deliverables:

    • Brand idenity

    • Stage Design

    • Physical 8'x10' Backdrop

The band Crosley Court desired to create a stage backdrop that could help brand themselves in a bold and styalistic manner. By working collaboratively with the artist, the idea of having a 70's style font and a painted style gradient background came into fruition.

My role was to construct the typography, find a local painter, and communicate with the printing company.
